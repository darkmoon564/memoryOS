# CEO Agent Demo Questions

Use these in order for a coherent demo story:

1. **What should I prioritize this week?**
2. **What is putting our Q3 revenue plan at risk?**
3. **Which customer needs my attention, and why?**
4. **What did Northstar ask us to prove before the next meeting?**
5. **Should we keep the July enterprise launch date?**
6. **What are the most important engineering blockers?**
7. **How are MRR, retention, and runway trending?**
8. **Summarize the decision we made about Cloudflare expansion.**

For a strong closing question, ask: **What should I tell the board on Friday?**

## Demo flow

Open the CEO briefing, ask question 1, then move to Company memory and ask questions 3–6. Finish by capturing a new note such as: *"Northstar confirmed a follow-up meeting for August 2 after we share the updated enterprise retention plan."* Then ask: *"What changed since the last investor meeting?*
